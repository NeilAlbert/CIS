Welcome to Covenant International School – Dawhenya Branch.

At our school, we are committed to raising disciplined, knowledgeable, and God-fearing students. Our mission is to combine academic excellence with strong moral values, ensuring every child reaches their full potential.

We believe in the principle "Work and Pray", guiding our students to succeed both academically and spiritually.

Who We Are

About Us

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures academic excellence, strong moral values, and spiritual growth in every student, preparing them for a successful and purposeful life.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Ghana, producing well-rounded graduates who are intellectually capable, morally upright, and spiritually grounded.

Our History

Covenant International School – Dawhenya Branch was founded with a passionate vision to bring quality, values-based education to the Dawhenya community in Greater Accra. Since its establishment, the school has grown steadily, earning a reputation for discipline, academic achievement, and spiritual integrity.

Core Values

Discipline

We cultivate self-control, respect, and responsibility in all our students.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics, character, and service.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all we do.

Faith

We encourage spiritual growth and a deep reverence for God in daily life.

Health & Safety

Our school maintains strict health protocols to ensure the safety of every student and staff member.

Transportation

For transport inquiries and pick-up/drop-off arrangements, please contact the school office.

Feeding Programme

Students benefit from nutritious meals through our school feeding programme during the academic day.

School Policies

Uniform Policy

All students must wear the official school uniform on all school days. Smart appearance reflects our values.

Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is compulsory. Parents must notify the school of any absences before 8:00 AM.

Discipline Policy

We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for bullying and misconduct. Discipline is handled with firmness and fairness.

Device & Phone Policy

Mobile phones are not permitted during school hours. All electronic devices must be approved by the school.
